What is express Delivery

Express Delivery is applicable only on products pre-selected as 'Express Delivery' at the time of checkout. For 'Standard Delivery', products
will be delivered according to the times specified on the checkout page.

If the order is placed and confirmed before 12 pm (noon), orders that qualify for Express Delivery will be fulfilled within the same calendar
day, If the order is placed and confirmed after 12 pm (noon), orders that qualify for Express Delivery will be fulfilled within the next calendar
day.
